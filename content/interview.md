# Interview Q&A

## What drives you as an engineer?

I'm motivated by building things that are useful. The best software disappears into the background — it just works, and people's lives are a little better because of it. I care about craft, but craft in service of outcomes, not for its own sake.

## What was your path at C3.ai?

I joined C3.ai as a Forward Deployed Engineer, working directly with enterprise customers to build AI applications on the C3 platform. I progressed to Senior FDE, where I took on more technical leadership and architecture ownership, and then to Manager of Forward Deployed Engineering, where I led a team delivering customer-facing AI solutions.

## What differentiates you?

I operate comfortably across the full stack — from data pipelines and ML models to frontend interfaces and deployment infrastructure. But what really sets me apart is the ability to translate between technical and business contexts. As an FDE, I had to deeply understand customer problems AND build the technical solutions. That dual perspective is rare and valuable.

## Why side projects?

Side projects are where I get to build exactly what I want, the way I want to build it. Beach League and GiftWell both started as real problems I wanted to solve. They keep my product instincts sharp and let me experiment with new technologies in a low-stakes environment.
